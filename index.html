A premium residential high-rise designed around balancing privacy, community, and wellness.

Symphony Heights is a contemporary luxury residential tower that focuses on spacious living, privacy, and premium community spaces. The design philosophy revolves around one concept: Rhythm of Life. This means every aspect of daily living has its own dedicated space.


Instead of mixing all amenities together, the project divides experiences into three distinct levels—Ground, Podium, and Rooftop—ensuring that each level supports a different mood while keeping residential areas completely private.

Elevated Landscaped Walkway

One of the unique features of Symphony Heights. Rather than walking beside roads, residents use an elevated landscaped pedestrian bridge connecting different amenities. It is a completely vehicle-free, safe, and pet-friendly zone fostering community interaction.

Cascading Deck Architecture

Instead of flat balconies, every apartment has staggered decks creating a cascading appearance on the façade. This offers better privacy, more sunlight, larger outdoor seating areas, and establishes a dynamic, layered architectural rhythm.

Exclusive Unit Plans

Designed with minimal shared walls, large windows, and open layouts. The interiors follow a warm modern luxury aesthetic with wooden finishes and marble flooring.
